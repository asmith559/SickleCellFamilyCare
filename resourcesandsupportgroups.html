Additional Community Programs

  • Peer Mentorship Programs: Learn from individuals living with SCD or caregivers.
  • Youth & Teen Programs: Empowering younger people through camps, clubs, and creative outlets.
  • Caregiver Support Networks: Encouragement and advice for those providing care.
  • Family Events: Regular gatherings to build community and share experiences.

Helpful Links & Hotlines

When you need quick answers or emergency support, these trusted services are here for you.

National Organizations

  • Sickle Cell Disease Association of America (SCDAA): https://www.sicklecelldisease.org
  • CDC Sickle Cell Resources: https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/sicklecell
  • American Society of Hematology: ASH Sickle Cell Resources
  • National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute: NHLBI Sickle Cell Information

Hotlines & Services

  • 24/7 Nurse Helplines: Contact your insurance provider or state services.
  • Transportation Assistance Programs: Help with getting to medical appointments.
  • Patient Advocacy Hotline: 1-800-555-7890
  • Crisis Support Line: 1-888-372-1000
